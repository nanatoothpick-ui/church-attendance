Connect to Google Sheets

This app saves all attendance to a Google Sheet — free, works on any device, and you can share it with your church leaders. Follow these steps once:

1

Open Google Sheets and create a new spreadsheet. Name it "Church Attendance".

2

Click Extensions → Apps Script. Delete everything in the editor.

3

Open the file google-apps-script.js included with this app. Copy its entire contents and paste into Apps Script.

4

Click Deploy → New deployment. Choose type Web app. Set "Who has access" to Anyone. Click Deploy and copy the URL.

5

Paste that URL below and click Connect.

Your data goes only to your own Google Sheet.
